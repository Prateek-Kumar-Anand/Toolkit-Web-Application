                      Text → Editable PDF

                      Upload a photo of text (notes, a printed page, a whiteboard) and OCR reads it, right in your browser. Check the recognized text for mistakes, fix anything it got wrong, then export it as a real PDF with selectable, editable text — not just a picture.

                      Share File

                      Pick a PDF, photo, video, or any other file and hand it straight to your device's native share sheet — AirDrop, Bluetooth, Messages, Mail, WhatsApp, whatever you've got. Photos and videos get an instant preview so you can confirm what you're sending, PDFs are checked for corruption before you send them, and if native sharing isn't available it always falls back to a clean download instead — nothing is ever uploaded to a server.

                      Drag & drop a file here, or click Choose File(s) above.
